ABOUT Touch One Life

To create positive and personal differences in the view of the most vulnerable and less fortunate members of the society

The vulnerable and less fortunate members of the society include:

  • The orphans
  • The widows and widowers
  • The handicapped
  • The street families
  • The prisoners
  • The eldery

To uplift such vulnerable people group so that they can achieve productive lives; living with healthy habits and attitude towards self and others.

These and other charitable programmes will influence the development of compassion, profitable choices, positive mental anitudes and mind confidence.
